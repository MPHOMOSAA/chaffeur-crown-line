The Crown Line Story

From Passion
to Prestige

Crown Line Chauffeurs was born from one man's deep love for cars. Bandile Madikizela, a seasoned entrepreneur and investor, built this brand on an unshakeable passion for automobiles — their power, elegance, and the extraordinary feeling they create.

In 2014, Bandile turned that passion into purpose — founding Crown Line Chauffeurs and building it into one of South Africa's most trusted luxury transport brands. He wanted to give everyone the chance to experience the thrill of being driven in a world-class vehicle. Whether it's a matric dance, a wedding, or a corporate transfer, Crown Line delivers a royal experience every time.

Today — over 10 years since its founding — Crown Line operates across Cape Town, Johannesburg and KwaZulu-Natal with a hand-picked fleet of premium vehicles. A proven name in luxury transport, trusted by matric students, executives, and wedding parties across South Africa.
