The Crown Line Story

From Passion to
Prestige

Crown Line Chauffeurs was born from one man's deep love for cars. Bandile Madikizela, the founder and driving force behind the brand, grew up with an unshakeable passion for automobiles — their power, their elegance, and the feeling they create.

In 2023, Bandile turned that passion into purpose. He wanted to give everyone — not just the ultra-wealthy — a chance to experience the thrill and luxury of being driven in a world-class vehicle. Whether it's a matric dance, a wedding, a corporate event, or simply a journey in style, Crown Line Chauffeurs delivers that royal experience every single time.

Today, Crown Line operates across Cape Town, Johannesburg and KwaZulu-Natal — with a hand-picked fleet of premium vehicles, a team of professional chauffeurs, and an unwavering commitment to making every ride extraordinary.
